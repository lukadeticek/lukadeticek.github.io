About Me

My name is Luka Detiček and I study at the Faculty of Natural Sciences and Engineering at the University of Ljubljana. I have been ‘into’ photography since I was a young boy, when my father took an old Canon camera with AA batteries and I played with it. I started to take photography more seriously in high school, where I became fascinated by portrait photography and the feeling that it can evoke in the observer. I have been doing photography for a little over 3 years now. When I take photographs, my mind drifts to the world beyond, as can be seen in the photographs above.
