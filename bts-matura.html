PROJECT: 30 Years Of Matura

BTS of a project we did for RIC. We recorded the event celebrating 30 years since Matura was reinstated in Slovenia Gymnasiums and Secondary Schools. The event was really well coordinated. The photos are some of my favourite BTS photos I've ever taken. I tried to replicate the widelux look, with the fisheye and 2:1 crop.
